Ladakh, India

Ladakh is a high-altitude desert located in the northernmost region of India. Known for its stunning landscapes, monasteries, and beautiful lakes like Pangong and Tso Moriri, it's a destination for adventure lovers and spiritual seekers alike. The unique culture and scenery are breathtaking.
